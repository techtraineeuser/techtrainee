General Questions

What is TechTrainee and what courses do you offer?

TechTrainee offers 5 specialized courses: ASP.NET Core with C#, Django (Python), Automation Testing, Business Intelligence (Data Analysis), and Frontend Development. All courses are 3-month intensive programs designed to make you job-ready.

Do you offer job guarantee with your courses?

Yes, we offer 100% job guarantee! This includes interview preparation, resume building, job placement assistance, and support until you get placed. Our success rate is over 95%.

What are the eligibility criteria for your courses?

We welcome candidates from all backgrounds - any degree/diploma, year gap candidates, non-IT background, and working professionals. No prior programming experience is required as we start from basics.
